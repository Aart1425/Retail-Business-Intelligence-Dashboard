# 📊 Retail Sales & Growth Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive insights into retail sales performance through interactive KPIs, dynamic filtering, and business intelligence visualizations.

---

## Dashboard Features

- Total Revenue KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Units Sold
- Revenue by Product
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Sub-Category
- Monthly Gross Profit Trend
- Revenue Waterfall Analysis
- Interactive Filters
  - Country
  - Year
  - Month
  - Quarter
